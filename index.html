History of The Uke Shop

The Uke Shop was founded in 1996 by John and Jane Doe. They handcrafted their ukeleles from the wood from the trees in their backyard. Now, the company has grown into in a worldwide supplier of ukeleles and is run by Jonah Doe, their son.
